(Not) Lost in Translation

WeebCoin is designed to reward and encourage translation efforts for their work in facilitating cultural exchange.

Designed by weebs, for weebs

We know that trying to buy products directly from Japan can be difficult, and that buying products from a specialized dealer in imported Japanese goods can often be much more expensive than the original price in Yen. WeebCoin aims to streamline the process of importing products from Japan by eliminating the middleman and benefitting both overseas buyers and content creators in Japan.

Transactions you can trust

One issue prevalent among independent Japanese content creators is a lack of trust in Western fans that want to colloborate or purchase assets for use in derivative works. The concerns over how money should be divided and split up alone can cause some otherwise great project ideas to fail. WeebCoin uses smart contracts to ensure that the way funds are divided between collaborators is mutually understood and able to be trusted.
